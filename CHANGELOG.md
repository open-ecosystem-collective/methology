## v1.0 – 2026

**Initial release of the complete methodology.**

### Assessments
- `assessments/partner-ecosystem-maturity-assessment-v1.0.md` – Full assessment with 5 dimensions, 15 questions, 75-point scoring
- `assessments/scoring-guide.md` – Interpretation guide, score bands, typical profiles

### Playbooks
- `playbooks/mdf-application-guide.md` – Step-by-step MDF application for major vendors
- `playbooks/co-sell-framework.md` – How to structure co-sell with software vendors
- `playbooks/partner-tier-structure.md` – How to design a partner tier structure for your company

### Templates
- `templates/discovery-report-template.md` – Output template for Level 1 engagements
- `templates/assessment-report-template.md` – Output template for Level 2 engagements
- `templates/collaboration-plan-template.md` – Output template for Level 3 engagements
